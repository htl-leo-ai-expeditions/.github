# 🚀 HTL Leo AI Expeditions

Welcome to the **HTL Leo AI Expeditions** GitHub organization! We are a group of students and educators from [HTL Leonding](https://www.htl-leonding.at/) exploring the exciting world of **Artificial Intelligence**.

## 🧭 What We Do

We run hands-on AI projects — *expeditions* — where students dive deep into real-world machine learning, data science, and AI engineering challenges. Each expedition is a learning journey that combines theory with practical implementation.

## 📚 Our Expeditions

Browse our repositories to find projects covering topics such as:

- 🤖 Machine Learning & Deep Learning
- 🗣️ Natural Language Processing
- 👁️ Computer Vision
- 📊 Data Science & Analytics
- 🔧 AI Engineering & MLOps

## 🤝 Get Involved

We welcome contributions, feedback, and collaboration from the community. Feel free to explore our repositories, open issues, or reach out!
